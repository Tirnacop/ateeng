Motivation

Sexism awareness – women represented gentle human beings, but they were seen as objects or slaves. Makeup and clothing were empowering the woman.

Fashion industry in England around XVII th to XX th is a reference for today’s fashion codes.

It is necessary to know about this subject in order to learn from the past and apply what we have learnt in the present. 

Introduction

From the Victorian period to the civil war and the 90s, the unique styles of England have evolved and they represented a point of orientation for other countries.

The fashion industry goes hand in hand with historical events over time.

depending on the specific events of the past times, the influences on the clothing and presentation in society have modified in conformity with the required codes.
